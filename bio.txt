Nicolas Garcia Belmonte works as a Staff Data Scientist and is the
Technical Lead for Data Visualization at Twitter. Nicolas leads Twitter
interactive, a platform that delivers public-facing data visualizations
around verticals like News, Government, Sports and TV in collaboration
with the Comms and Media teams. Prior to joining Twitter, Nicolas
created open source frameworks for advanced data visualization that were
used by organizations like Mozilla, Google, the White House and Twitter.
As a speaker, Nicolas participated in international conferences doing
presentations in english, french and spanish. His work has been cited
in multiple books including Data Points by Nathan Yau. Today his interests
range from advanced techniques in graphics to investigative journalism.
