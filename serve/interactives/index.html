Interactives

At Twitter we’ve been releasing small interactive data visualizations that usually provide context or complement blog posts.

We created a visualization of most mentioned #DoctorWho Tweets for the 50yr Doctor Who anniversary.

Also there’s Simon Rogers’ blog post about Twitter’s biggest moments which was complemented with an interactive comparing biggest moments.

We also worked on an interactive for the Emmys. This interactive shows TV shows by category, plus total and average number of Tweets per episode. Here’s the blog post also done by the Twitter media team.

Most the work here is generally done within the week, having these as “small” visualizations that complement stories.
