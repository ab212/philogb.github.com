Summary

I lead the Data Visualization Team at Uber. Previously I was Staff Data Scientist and Technical Lead for Data Visualization at Twitter, leading Twitter Interactive, a platform that delivers public-facing data visualizations around verticals like News, Government, Sports and TV in collaboration with Twitter's Comms and Media teams. Prior to joining Twitter, I created several open source frameworks for advanced data visualization, like the JavaScript Infovis Toolkit and PhiloGL, that were used by organizations like Mozilla, Google, the White House and Twitter. Today my interests range from advanced techniques in graphics to investigative journalism.
