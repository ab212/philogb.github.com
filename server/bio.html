Nicolas Garcia Belmonte works as a data visualization scientist at Twitter, where he provides knowledge in graphics and information visualization to different teams. Nicolas also leads the #interactive effort that sets a public-facing platform for data visualizations by Twitter to the Web. Among other projects Nicolas authored the visualizations presented by Twitter's CEO Dick Costolo during the IPO roadshow. Nicolas' WebGL and JavaScript libraries have been used by companies like Google, Mozilla and Twitter. His visualizations were featured in blogs like FlowingData and FastCompany and in books like Nathan Yau's Visualize This. Nicolas has been a speaker in several conferences on graphics, storytelling and data visualization. You can find more about his work on data visualization at his home page: http://philogb.github.io/
